Biography

Xing Xu is a lecturer with Center of Future Media & School of Computer Science and Engineering, University of Electronic of Science and Technology of China (UESTC), China. He received the B.E. and M.E. degrees from Huazhong University of Science and Technology, China, in 2009 and 2012, respectively, and the Ph.D. degree from Kyushu University, Japan, in 2015. Before joining UESTC, he worked as a Research Technician at Kyushu University (2015-2016). He received the best paper award at the 25th ACM International Conference on Multimedia (MM 2017), the World’s FIRST 10K Best Paper Award-Platinum Award from the 18th IEEE International Conference on Multimedia & Expo (ICME 2017), the best student paper award from the 28th edition of the Australasian Database Conference (ADC 2017), and the excellent poster award from the 21th Japan-Korea Joint Workshop on Frontiers of Computer Vision (FCV 2015). His current research interests mainly focus on multimedia information retrieval, pattern recognition and computer vision. He has served as a reviewer for IEEE TMM, IEEE TIP, PR, Neurocomputing and guest editor for Multimedia Tools and Application.
